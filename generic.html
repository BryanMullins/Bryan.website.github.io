About Me

Twenty-five year old from Ireland relocating to New York City in April on a J1 Graduate Visa.

After graduating with a First Class Honours in Business Analytics from the University of Galway. I went on to work as a Banking Analyst at Grant Thornton the sixth largest U.S. accounting and advisory firm.

My position at Grant Thornton involved adopting a client facing role as the primary contact for both client and internal functions within assigned projects. I proactively interacted with client management to gather information, resolve issues, and provide analysis to inform key client deliverables.

I had to contribute to internal reporting requirements including the preparation of operational reports, management updates and client presentations. For this position demonstrating excellent attention to detail and an ability to work to strict deadlines was required.

During my time with Grant Thornton, I also got numerous opportunities to go on secondment and work with various clients such as Finance Ireland and Pepper Asset Servicing. At Pepper I was involved in the migration of Ulster Bank accounts to Permanent TSB after the acquisition of the bank was complete. I also got the opportunity to work with their HR department on ‘Project Accelerate’ which involved analysing the progress of new hires to improve employee retention.
